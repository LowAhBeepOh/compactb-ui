Privacy

Data Encryption

When using a CompactB Module Block connected to nearby users, all communications are secured using Budflick Encryption to ensure maximum privacy and security. For personal CompactB Module Block connections, we implement basic encryption protocols to protect your data. Cloud server communications utilize your browser's standard HTTPS encryption. Local responses processed directly on your PC are unencrypted for optimal performance.

Data Handling

Your privacy is our priority. All data stored on our cloud servers is automatically deleted at the end of each session, ensuring your conversations remain private. We maintain a strict no-third-party-sharing policy to keep your data solely between you and CompactB.

For model training purposes, chats are automatically saved when you're using a personal CompactB Module Block or using our local model through the website. However, if you're using public CompactB Module Blocks or cloud servers, you're automatically opted out from contributing your chats to model training. We respect your choice and privacy, and we won't sell your data either.

Cookie Policy

We keep our cookie usage minimal and transparent. Only essential cookies necessary for basic functionality are enabled by default. Analytics cookies are optional and can be enabled or disabled according to your preference. We do not use any tracking or data collection cookies, maintaining your browsing privacy.
